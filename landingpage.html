Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Lineum Hackathon

Do I need to be a developer to participate?

No! While developers are crucial, we also need designers, artists, strategists, and domain experts. The best projects are multidisciplinary.

Is there a cost to participate?

No, the hackathon is completely free to join. We believe in removing barriers to participation.

Can I participate as an individual?

Yes, you can participate as an individual or as part of a team (up to 5 members). We'll also have team formation channels in our Discord.

What do I need to prepare?

Just your skills and enthusiasm! We recommend familiarizing yourself with basic web3 concepts, but we'll have resources and mentors available.
